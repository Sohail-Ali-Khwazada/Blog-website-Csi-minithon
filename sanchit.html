Sanchit Shokeen

Sanchit Shokeen is a young and ambitious entrepreneur who embarked on his journey to share technical knowledge through video explanations on his YouTube channel, TechBar. After completing his engineering degree, he pursued his dream of helping ordinary people make informed choices when purchasing gadgets. Over the past nine years, Sanchit has created over 800 videos, gaining millions of subscribers with his straightforward and jargon-free approach to reviewing a wide range of gadgets, from smartphones and gaming consoles to laptops and smartwatches. His unique video ideas, such as visiting service centers and demystifying flash sales, have set him apart in the tech review world. Sanchit prides himself on providing honest and realistic reviews to assist viewers in making wise decisions. His success story serves as an inspiration to millennials looking to pursue their passions and entrepreneurial dreams. Through TechBar, Sanchit continues to empower his audience with valuable knowledge while enjoying the fruits of his hard work, including purchasing his own car with the earnings from his videos.
