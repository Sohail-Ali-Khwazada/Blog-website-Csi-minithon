Shlok Srivastava

Srivastava started making videos for YouTube around eight years ago. Now, his channel Tech Burner, which focuses on gadget reviews, how-to, and life hacks, has accumulated over 8.3 million subscribers and millions in monthly viewerships. He previously posted in English, but switched to Hindi. Srivastava also built a digital media company around the channel, managing two websites and social media accounts on Instagram and Facebook, covering tech and gadgets. Recently, Srivastava launched a clothing line brand, Overlays.Making tech simple and majedaar [fun] one video at a time is Shlok Srivastava's motto. He has garnered 8.78 million subscribers on his YouTube channel. Last year, he created a swanky new studio and office for his content creation, and has come a long way from when he started on YouTube in September 2014. Videos of the 2021 alumnus of Forbes India 30 Under 30 have cumulatively amassed over 1.21 billion views so far.
